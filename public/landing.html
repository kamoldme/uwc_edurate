Features

Everything your school needs

🔒

Secure & Anonymous

Verified school email system ensures only real students submit — with full anonymity.

⭐

6-Criteria Ratings

Rate teachers on clarity, engagement, fairness, supportiveness, preparation, and workload.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Teachers see aggregated scores with detailed charts, trends, and period-over-period progress.

Content Moderation

Admin-reviewed content moderation with approval workflows keeps feedback constructive.

Term Management

Organize feedback by academic terms with controlled feedback periods and schedules.

Department Support

Organize teachers by department for structured feedback and targeted analytics.

Rich Feedback

Star ratings, written comments, and customizable tags for detailed, actionable insights.

Audit Logging

Complete activity tracking across the platform for transparency and accountability.
