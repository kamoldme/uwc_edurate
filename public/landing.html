How It Works

Simple for everyone involved

Three easy steps to transform teaching through the power of student voice.

01
🏫

Students Join Classrooms

Use a unique join code to enroll in your teacher's classroom. No personal information is ever shared or exposed.

02
💬

Submit Honest Feedback

Rate your teacher with a simple 5-star rating and leave written comments — fully anonymous to your teacher and classmates.

03
📈

Teachers Improve

Teachers see aggregated scores and trends to understand what is working and where to grow, term after term.

Features

Everything your school needs

Purpose-built for the UWC community, with every tool that matters for meaningful feedback.

Secure & Anonymous

Verified school email system ensures only real students submit — with full anonymity guaranteed.

⭐

6-Criteria Ratings

Rate teachers on clarity, engagement, fairness, supportiveness, preparation, and workload management.
